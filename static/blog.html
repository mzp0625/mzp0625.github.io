Why Computer Science

  • Author: Mark Ma
  • Created: Sept 10, 2019

Before I started school at USC, I was first a student in civil engineering at UC Berkeley. I loved math and the physical sciences, and understood the importance computing technology plays in a traditional field of engineering.

As I entered the workplace as a structural engineer, I realized that instead of drawings, calculations and coordination meetings, my passion in engineering had always been the more technical and analytical aspect. I wanted to be part of the teams of computer scientists that create the tools which would revolutionize many of the industries in the world today. I knew I would not learn simply by using software tools created by others.

Leaving a stable job that I have been working for many years had not been easy, even though I have rehearsed the scenario many times in my mind. However looking back, I do not regret it one bit - I now have the opportunity to join the industry which will reshape the society for the upcoming decades.
